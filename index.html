Frequently Asked Questions

Is my legal ownership guaranteed?

Yes. Every investment is backed by a legal deed and trust structure. You receive a digital certificate of ownership that corresponds to your exact fractional share of the physical land.

How can I exit or sell my shares?

You can sell your shares on our secondary market to other investors, or wait for the project's maturity date when the land is sold to a developer and profits are distributed to all shareholders.

Can I physically visit the land?

Absolutely. We encourage it. We organize regular site visits for our investors so you can see exactly where your money is invested.

How does Aamar Land make money?

We charge a small, transparent sourcing fee when you invest, and a performance fee only when you make a profit upon exiting. There are no hidden charges.
